# 更改邮箱（Email）

**第一步**：在导航栏内找到`Profile`→`Edit My Profile`

![](https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/acidandsalt/ohsnap/site\_1/j1.jpg)

**第二步**：点击`Change Email`

![](https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/acidandsalt/ohsnap/site\_1/Screenshot\_20230309\_193854.jpg)

**第三步**：填写`New Email`，`Confirm New Email`和`Password`，点击下方`Change Email`确认。

![](https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/acidandsalt/ohsnap/site\_1/MTXX\_MH20230309\_194132571.jpg)
